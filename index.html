How It Works

This cyberbullying detector uses advanced pattern recognition to analyze text for harmful content including:

  • Threats & Violence: Direct or implied threats of harm
  • Harassment: Repeated hostile or aggressive behavior
  • Discrimination: Content targeting protected characteristics
  • Exclusion: Language designed to isolate or ostracize
  • Offensive Language: Profanity and derogatory terms

Privacy Notice

All text analysis is performed locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.
